LOVE LETTER TYPEWRITER was created from the scanned pages 
of love letters written from my grandfather to my 
grandmother in the late 1930s and early 1940s (she replied 
in blue ink). There were no samples of a few of the 
characters, so these were taken from fresh 
imprints of other manual typewriters of similar vintage.
