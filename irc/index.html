Neocities seemingly has its own unofficial IRC channel that has been used on and off for some time now, however, there is very little in the terms of past chat logs that have been saved and made public, that's why this page was set up as a way to hopefully get a growing archive.

Much like with my old archive of NeoMail, I will not be adding any comments or corrections to what people have written as the chats are a time capture of the time they were written - the only edits I have made is removing any IP addresses that may appear.

If you have any logs from #neocities, then please join the NeoZones Discord guild and post them there or send a PR to the owly.fans git. If you wish to join the chatroom, click this link (or join via your browser).

This page also has logs for #neozones on Libera Chat, if you want to join that room, click this link (or here via browser).
