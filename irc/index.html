Neocities seemingly has its own unofficial IRC channel that has been used on and off for some time now, however, there is very little in the terms of past chat logs, that's why this page was set up as a way to hopefully get a growing archive.

Much like with my old archive of NeoMail, I will not be adding any comments or corrections to what people have written as the chats are a time capture of the time they were written - the only edits I have made is removing any IP addresses that may appear.

If you have any logs from #neocities, then please join the NeoZones Discord server and post them there. If you wish to, for somereason, join this chatroom, click this link.
