Oh dear. Do you want to see something a little naff? Yeah you do, come on now! As I didn't want to just wing it and not know what I was going to draw, I went ahead and made what I wanted to make in MS Paint of all things (MS Paint on Windows XP, mind you). As you can see, the colors are a little different as a whole, like the fox's was going to be this darkish-pinkish color with blue ear tuffs. Yeah, glad that I opted to scrap that idea in favor of the final thing.

Attached is the poster that was put into the final art. Note that the smiley face has a black outline while the final thing does not. I don't know how I feel about the face being the only thing in the art to not have an outline, but it is what it is.

Also, I am very aware that these two images look crap.
