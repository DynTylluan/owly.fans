Ranking Every Vivzipop Song

Well gamers, it's time, it's time for me to do the unthinkable, I am going to rank every Vivzipop song, from Helluva Boss to Hazbin Hotel and other random stuff,we're going to have a fun old time. Or not!

Worst to best...

I'm Always Chasing Rainbows

As sung by ???, this is the opaning track for the Hazbin Hotel poilet. For the firts song, yeah, it's a good one for the most part, if not a little... boring, I guess around the middle of it, but it's a fine start, plus it's the only song on this list that is a cover. Also, with the theming of the charater who is singing, Charlie, the vocals are quite sweet sounding and can be real good.

Overall, it's a good cover and a very okay start to the poilet for the show, but isn't anything amazing or groundbreaking, plus others have made better covers of it throughtout the years. I think the main reason why it's so low on this list is just becasue I find it so boring and I don't think it offers me anything new in terms of music.

I really feel mean for putting this song so low as it is one of the first times (or the first?) that we hear in Hazbin Hotel that is an orignal Vivzipop song, and while it has a lot of fun parts to it, I just feel like this song is nothing more than a nothingburger and isn't all that fun to play to myself. If I heard this on the radio, I would flip to another station.

If it means anything, I like certan parts to the music video for this song, like how Charlie goes full demon for a good few seconds is cool, for sure, but as I said, I just don't care all that much for this song, I just don't think it's all that amazing.

I feel like I have a very love-hate relationship with this song as a whole - sure there are some good parts, but others are just kind of naff. With that said, I do like the singing for what it's worth, but overall, this sounds like something I would have heard on the radio about a decade ago.

In the song, there's also a second mini-song when suddaly Verosika is the focas of the track in what I can only think is a soft-rock like tune. Verosika as a singer is underrated in Helluva Boss and I feel like we're really missing out on some action from her.

Overall, this is a great track that really shows me the progression of the show and how it has changed for the better.
