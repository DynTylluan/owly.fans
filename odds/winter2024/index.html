Meta: OwlyFans' Winter 2024 Theme

Back in the bad old days of the 2000s, I was a Club Penguin addict. I'd get back home from school, boot up the family computer that was located in the dining room, and go on to my favorite website. I had hours of fun playing as a simple light blue penguin. One thing that I loved about Club Penguin was simply that the little town that the residents lived in would, at times, change the theme and put up little decorations. There would be an April Fools theme where everything became whacky, a St. Patrick's Day, Easter, and my favorrite: Halloween.
