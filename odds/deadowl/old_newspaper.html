OUR FRIEND, THE VIEWER

This week, Viking reported on an up-coming police report on the so-called 'cult', Fellowship of The Dead Owl, after out report came out, a large number of people wrote to us, telling us what they think about this report.

Sally Jones from North Hull writes in by telling us
Sir, I think that the police are doing the best they can, they have enough trouble with gang crime in England, I'm sure that they are doing their best to look into this[.]

While Max Python from Upper Grim seems very hard on the Force
Sir, it should be the job of the police to stop any crime they see fit, if they don't do anything soon, them I am more than sure that if they don't do anything, then the strong arm will have blood on their hands. I was apart of the Force for more than 30 years, and in that time Sir Whitehouse [ex-head of The Police Force UK] did his very best in stopping crime, but now it would seem that Mis. Cox is doing her worst. In no time the UK will go the the pigs!

Strong words from Max Pythong there. Rich Hunt from London seems to think that the cult have something to do with the up-rise of computers and the World Wide Web
Sir, it is no surprise that an evil cult like this has been made. My grand-son, Harry, owns a PC Computer, and because of it, he is able to commit a number of crimes! He told me that for only £32 he can pay someone to kill a person, for £20 he can buy a number of drugs, and thanks to computer games, he is also able to play such games as "Murder Quest 3000"! My word, what a horrible world we like in.
