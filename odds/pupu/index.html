In October 2018 I found a Finnish comic by the name of Ahistunu Pupu - the only problem was that I don't speak Finnish, I can only speak English. So as a way of saying sorry for Brexit, I translated the comic into my own language... Using only Google Translate...

There was only one big problem; getting computers to translate can be a mixed bag. For translating some comics, the Finnish may have made sense, but the English sure didn't, because of this, I took some liberties in the new comics. I hope that I did my best, and even if the comic has a new meaning, I hope that they are still fun to read.
