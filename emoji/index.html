Emoji Index

Rather than relying on the end user's device to render their own emojis and have the risk of them not showing up the way that I would want them to, I instead opted to use emojis that I hand-picked myself. I don't want to write all that much on this page as you can read about it on the git, but most of the emojis on this site have been taken from Mutant Standard, an open-source emoji pack created and designed by Dzuk, being released into the Creative Commons, with a number of edits made by myself.

I have also opted to use other emoji sets, such as Twemoji and others that you should read about in the credit section.
