The license on this page has now been surpassed by version 1.0 - you might like to use that version instead of this one.

The "DVB license" which the transpiler and all side projects were licensed under is one of the most restrictive open source licenses ever conceived. It was designed by the developers as a way to release source code without the risk of it being used or shared by anyone. The only right given to the recipient is the right to be sued. However no lawsuits were brought against recipients of the DVB license in the life of the project.
