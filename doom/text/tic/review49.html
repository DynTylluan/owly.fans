 Review date: 11. June 95                            Review No.:  49
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: The WAD                  Game: Doom 2
 Filename: THE.ZIP                  Size: 98k

   Author: W.Davis Thornton       e-mail: William_Thornton@baylor.edu

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: yes, walls
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Very well constructed level,  looks like one of the later original
Doom 2  levels -  city-style with  buildings etc.  A few nice ideas,
such as a church,  a cinema and a cemetary.  All keys were  used and
well placed.  Good light usage.  The author  wanted to  make  a good
deathmatch-level too - I think it's much better for single and co-op
games.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Well placed and  aligned textures,  only a few misalignments.  The
new textures are nice,  but unnecessary  (except for the cinema).  A
teleporter does not have the right texture, just a red floor.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Very challenging,  playing in  Ultra Violence  you have  to kill a
Cyberdemon.  A lot of Imps,  Demons,  a few Mancubus' and Revenants.
Well placed.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo and health,  you will get it exactly when you need it, 
so I call this 'well placed'. A kinda difficult to get God-Mode, but
it's not necessary to kill the Cyberdemon.  Could be some more other
things, I can't remember seeing even some torches...

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  80%

  Worth  downloading.  Very  nice level,  too few  things  and  some
misaligned textures take 20%. Don't miss that one anyway.
