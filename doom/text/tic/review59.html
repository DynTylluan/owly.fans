Something of note

This WAD was made by Orin Flaharty, who in the same year this was written (1995), would also make two maps for Memento Mori (map05 and map08), a collaborative project that was, in part, made by The Innocent Crew - the same people who wrote these reviews.

This was also not the first review of one of his WADs by The Innocent Crew, the first being Moonbase.

 Review date:  7. July 95                            Review No.:  59
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Orin's 1st Doom 2 Level  Game: Doom 2
 Filename: ORIN01.ZIP               Size: 32k

   Author: Orin Flaharty          e-mail: akira963@aol.com

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A small to  medium sized level.  Very well constructed and thought
out. Very good light usage. 2 keys were used.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Only a few misaligned textures, not more than usual. :) Well done.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa or Tutti-Frutti effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Not very hard for  an expierenced  Doom 2 player,  but well placed
and sometimes tricky.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough health  and ammo.  Enough other  things,  such as  lamps or
hanging Arch-Viles. Well done.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  80%

  Worth downloading. Gives a nice Deathmatch-level too I think.
