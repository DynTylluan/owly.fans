 Review date: 14. May 95                             Review No.:  24
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Ace 1                    Game: Doom 2
 Filename: ACE1.ZIP                 Size: 20k

   Author: Unknown                e-mail: /

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Some rooms with a  few stairs and a  lot of teleporters.  Only one
key used  - not very  well planned  and much  too small  for a  good 
Doom 2 level. One-way level.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Not the best work, strange using of door-textures, some misaligned
textures etc.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tuttie-Frutti, Medusa or HOM effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Played in UV only. Much too easy, you will get a Plasma gun in a
'not to miss' secret. Too bad.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo and health, you will never be stuck on both of it.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  55%

  Worth downloading.  No level for  the advanced player,  too small,
too easy.
