 Review date:  7. July 95                            Review No.:  57
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Corridors Of Hell        Game: Doom 2
 Filename: COR_HEL2.ZIP             Size: 48k

   Author: Jens Nielsen           e-mail: djinni@ibm.net

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A medium to large sized level with a big labyrinth-like open area.
Plus  a waste  area  and a  special  arena-like  place with  lots of
monsters.  All three  keys were used.  The level  contains a  lot of
secrets,  you won't find all of them,  even if you have the automap.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Well  designed,  only a few  misaligned  textures.  Some doors are
moving into the sky - personally I don't like this.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa or Tutti-Frutti effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Very challenging,  at Ultra Violence  you will meet 4 Cyberdemons,
3 at one place,  together with some Arch-Viles.  It's hard,  but not
impossible - let them fight  eachother and don't  pass the blue door
if you don't have the Rocket Launcher & the Plasma Gun.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo and health,  but I think the  Rocket Launcher is a bit
misplaced. Enough other things. Well done.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  78%

  Worth downloading.
