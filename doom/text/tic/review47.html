 Review date: 7. June 95                             Review No.:  47
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Big_D                    Game: Doom 2
 Filename: BIG_D.ZIP                Size: 35k

   Author: Melvin R. Clemens III  e-mail: rick@map5621.fams.af.mil

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Medium sized  level with a few big rooms.  Good key-placement, all
three keys  were used.  Some areas are  a bit too dark,  such as the
computer-labyrinth, where the yellow key is. The whole level suffers
from some unclosed sectors,  the level is fully playable,  but some-
times it's a  bit confusing...  this is what  the author  calls: "No
bugs, just features". A few secrets.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Texture alignment is almost well done.  Some strange combinations,
but nothing really bad.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Very hard, a lot of Cacos, Arachnotrons and Mancubus'. In the room
with the red key is a Spider Mastermind. Overall a bit too hard.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  You will notice  a lack of ammo right  after the first room, later
you will get some weapons and ammo.  Enough health, one God-Mode and
a blue Sphere.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  55%

  Worth downloading;  kinda boring  and getting  the blue key can be
frustrating.  The bugs are unnecessary.  Download only if you need a
new level.
