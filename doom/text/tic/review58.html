 Review date:  7. July 95                            Review No.:  58
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: The Kurf House           Game: Doom 2
 Filename: KURFHSE.ZIP              Size: 94k

   Author: Colin L. Kurfess       e-mail: AOL

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: removed teleport-sound
 Graphics: some walls/signs
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A medium sized level,  it seems to be  a Doom 2 conversion  of his
house. Many nice things,  such as a  bathroom,  a bedroom,  a garage
with a car in it... Two keys were used. Good light usage.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  The whole  level  has  wooden  walls,  except the  garage  and the
cellar, looking very nice. The bedside rug is my favourite. :)

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa or Tutti-Frutti effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Not very  hard,  only a few  enemies  of any kind.  No Arch-Viles,
Cyberdemons or Spider-Masterminds.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough health and ammo. Only a few other things.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  72%

  Worth downloading. A level rather for watching than fighting.
