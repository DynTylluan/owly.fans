 Review date: 19. May 95                             Review No.:  35
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Who's afraid o.the dark? Game: Doom 2
 Filename: WAOTD.ZIP                Size: 98k

   Author: Joseph J. Pecoraro     e-mail: pecoraro@aol.com

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A huge level with large rooms and long hallways. Almost 90% of the
map is made  invisible, so you will  never know where you are.  This
sucks, because of a few labyrinths in this level. The goal is really
simple - run,  fire and stay in  front of closed doors.  Try to find
the key first... In addition the whole level is VERY dark and due to
its size slow.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  It's dark,  so you will not notice any misaligned textures.  But I
think it's almost well done.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties. I didn't see anything.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  You  will meet  a bunch of  Arch-Viles  in UV, this  sucks and  is
impossible.  The author played  it in Skill 3 and  added a demo LMP,
but it's  version 1.7 and converting  to 1.9 corrupted  it,  he dies
instantly. Many, many enemies of any kind.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Too many things  at all, this slows the  game down like hell. Just
imagine some stairs with about 40 lamps on it. Overdone.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  50%

  Worth downloading ONLY if you  like darkness and you really need a
new level.
