 Review date: 28. May 95                             Review No.:  39
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Adrenaline               Game: Doom 2
 Filename: ADRNILIN.ZIP             Size: 48k

   Author: Michael Fulbright      e-mail: msf@as.arizona.edu

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Small level,  only one key was used  and this one isn't  very well
placed.  The blue key  is right  before the  blue door,  luckily you
can't get it  without damage.  The level  is fully  one-way-like and
therefore kinda boring.  At the end  is a nice maze  with good light
usage, but all in all  the level suffers  from a lack of ideas. It's
the first Doom 2 level for  Michael and this is  what it looks like.
He just tried some things and I bet he's proud of what he did.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Almost every texture  is well placed,  only a few misaligned ones.
Also, well used  texture-combinations - the whole level  looks quite
good.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  A lot of monsters,  but well  placed and  very challenging.  But I
think the Barons and Knights at the end are unnecessary.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  A lot of ammo  and health,  this  doesn't  make the  level so much
easier, but fun. Although there is too much ammo and I think you are
able to get around 8 Blue Spheres. Not many other things...

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  63%

  Worth downloading.  But this is  really nothing special,  just one
of those  levels pushed out  by hundreds  of WAD-heros.  However the
level is much better than many many others out there.
