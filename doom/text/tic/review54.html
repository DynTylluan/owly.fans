 Review date: 18. June 95                            Review No.:  54
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: The Waterfront           Game: Doom 2
 Filename: WTRFRONT.ZIP             Size: 234k

   Author: Scott F. Crank         e-mail: scrank@holli.com

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: yes
 Graphics: yes
    Music: yes

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A medium sized level with an  excellent construction and many nice
tricks, e.g. the intended use of the Arch-Vile bug.  Some very funny
and sometimes  shocking  effects,  such as  animated  pictures  of a
second  player  or a  crusher-control-center.  Very nice  for single
player games,  great for  deathmatch-play as well.  Well placed keys
and good light usage. Overall a very innovative level...

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Excellent texture  placement and alignment,  only a few misaligned
lamps at walls. Enough said.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa or Tutti-Frutti effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Well placed enemies,  sometimes very challenging. The intended use
of the  Arch-Vile  bug can be  a bit annoying.  But not,  if you are
doing it right. :)

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Always enough ammo and health. Well done. Could be some more other
things around. Sometimes it looks a bit empty.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  86%

  Worth downloading. Don't miss that one!
