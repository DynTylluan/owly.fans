 Review date: 4. June 95                             Review No.:  45
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Bitchin7 - DeathZones    Game: Doom 2
 Filename: BITCHIN7.ZIP             Size: 324k

   Author: Bob Symonds            e-mail: 72752,1077

     Maps: MAP07                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: yes, weapons and some enemies
 Graphics: yes, doors, walls
    Music: taken from Doom-E3M3 (legal?)

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A big level  with large  hallways,  a lot of large  lifts and many 
huge rooms. The added graphics are limited to some warning signs and
some (kinda ugly) plain red walls. Nothing really amazing. All three
keys were used  and well placed.  The whole level  seems to have the 
same light level... boring.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Well aligned,  but all rooms are  128 or higher,  so there  are no
problems with alignment.  Except for some switches - you will notice 
that Bob isn't very familiar with texture-alignment. 

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Far too easy due to too few enemies;  also there are no difficulty 
settings, just Ultra Violence. Ultra boring.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Lots of ammo and health. Too few other things. Boring.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  50%

  Worth downloading,  only if you are  a beginner and  want some new
sounds. Otherwise, the ZIP-size isn't worth downloading for what you
will get.
