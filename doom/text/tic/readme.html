                          TiC's WAD Reviews
---------------------------------------------------------------------

          In irregular intervals I will review a few PWADs.
   All reviews are totally subjective, I'll not beautify anything!

    If you have any suggestions, ideas or whatever; e-mail me at:
      denis@doomsday.shnet.org or denis.moeller@kiel.netsurf.de

  ----------------------------------------------------------------

     I posted these 20 reviews to rec/games/computer/doom/playing
   and   alt/games/doom.   I  will  upload  every 20  reviews  to
   ftp.cdrom.com, just like this package, AFTER posting them.

     Included is a list of PWADs failed my rating system,  in the
   future I will  not review any PWADs  below 50% rating (by me).
   Also,  I'm reviewing  single  or multiplayer  levels only,  no
   deathmatch levels and no graphics or sound only PWADs.  I will
   test levels for Doom, Doom 2 and Heretic only.

     If you want me  to review a special PWAD  (if it is not good
   enough, I will add it to the bad PWADs list), e-mail me.

  ----------------------------------------------------------------

     A little  bit about me.  I'm Denis Moeller,  member  of TiC.
   TiC stands for The Innocent Crew.  We made 3 big Add-Ons and 2
   single Deathmatch levels for either Doom and Doom 2. Also, I'm
   the author of NWT, a very powerful tool for Doom.

     I played a lot of PWADs and I'm still looking for good ones.
   I downloaded all these PWADs and  was very angry getting about
   70% bullshit.  So I decided  to help other people  looking for
   nice PWADs by reviewing good ones and listing bad ones.
     I do attach great importance to a) logical construction (key
   placement), b) variety, c) texture design and d) gameplay.

   I really hope you like the reviews;  e-mail me what you think.
