 Review date: 1. May 95                              Review No.:   3
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Dark World I             Game: Doom
 Filename: DRKWRLD1.ZIP             Size: 25k

   Author: Stephen Holste         e-mail: Stephen.Holste@spectre.com

     Maps: E1M1                     Type: normal level
   Sounds: No
 Graphics: No
    Music: No

     Note: The author is 11 years old! Dad helped a bit.

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Labyrinth. A lot of rooms, hallways, a lot of doors - not designed
to look like doors. Really nothing special, (are there any different
floor levels?) boring.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  The whole level with one texture (okay, 98%) - some few misaligned
textures. 90% of the rooms have the same light level (medium). 

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tutti-Frutti or HOM effect. Doortraks are moving - argh!

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Played UV only. Very easy, no special traps or something, just one
enemy after another. Boring.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough health  and ammo.  Too easy.  No special  things to  make it 
more fun playing. Just to get, shoot, run and win. Boring.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  28%

  NOT worth downloading! Where is the idea? Every better Doom-player
can reach the end while sleeping at the same time.
