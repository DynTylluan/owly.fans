The Innocent Crew present*: TiC's WAD Reviews

*Kinda

This is an HTML version of old reviews from the early 1990s that I, Cass «Owly» Python, uploaded to my OwlyFans website. Previously, all of these reviews were submitted to the /idgames Archive, but as far as I'm aware, they were not uploaded anywhere else, so as a way of one, preserving Doom history and two, making it easy for people to read them, I made this small archive.

Enjoy. Oh, also this part of the website might not work all that well with screenreaders!

To be clear, none of the reviews were written by me, but The Innocent Crew (TiC), who, fun fact, also made Memento Mori.
