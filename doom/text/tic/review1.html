 Review date: 1. May 95                              Review No.:   1
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Illusions Of Home        Game: Doom
 Filename: ILUSORY1.ZIP             Size: 520k

   Author: Brett Librandi         e-mail: brianl@ilx.com

     Maps: E1M1-E3M9 (27)           Type: normal levels
   Sounds: No
 Graphics: No
    Music: No

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Mostly one way, a room, some stairs, some more rooms. Lifts, doors
etc., no special effects.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  A lot of misaligned  textures, extreme  use of light-effects (very
dark & blinking or very light).     Switches mostly don't fit, tele-
porters and other important floor & ceiling textures are wrong.
Door sizes are okay,  but the sides are moving,  sometimes a door is
opening into heaven.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  A few Tutti-Frutties. Many totally misaligned textures.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Enough,  but haphazardly  placed.  (Open a door,  kill them,  open
another, kill them all again etc.)

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough health,  too easy in UV.  Only a few  things placed  atmos-
pheric,  mostly  just  to  get  something.  Illogical  key-placement
(depends on level construction).

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  19%

  NOT worth downloading!  I recommend the  author to read  the 'Doom
Hacker's Guide' AND the WAD designers handbook.
