 Review date: 2. June 95                             Review No.:  44
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Below                    Game: Doom 2
 Filename: BELOW.ZIP                Size: 63k

   Author: Neil McKellar          e-mail: mckellar@cs.ualberta.ca

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A medium sized level with a lot of stairs. Overall kinda dark. The
main  idea is  the use  of stairs  and some  silver  columns.  Light
effects are  limited  to  flickering  torches,  all in  all  nothing
amazing.  Well placed keys - all keys  were used.  If you can't find
the red door, get back to the beginning.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Well aligned, I noticed that some  wall-lamps at doors are looking 
a bit strange, but the author tried to align them. No big mistakes.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  A lot of Shotgun Guys, a few Imps  and Heavy Weapon Dudes. Nothing
really challenging, only 3 Barons at the end. Not very hard.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo and health, could be some more other things.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  65%

  Worth downloading. A bit too dark I think and too easy.
