 Review date: 4. May 95                              Review No.:  18
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: MRDM (Series?)           Game: Doom
 Filename: MRDM2301.ZIP             Size: 45k

   Author: Michael Rapp           e-mail: michael.rapp@aquila.com

     Maps: E2M3                     Type: normal
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  YES! Very nice level.  Lots of rooms, ways, stairs, traps, windows
and a few lifts.  Two keys used.  Very good light usage at all.  The
whole level is very  symmetricaly constructed.  Good for two  player
games.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Only a few misaligned textures.  Clean work.  The author used very
good combinations of textures.  Every room has it's own athmosphere.
It is not  an evil level,  more a hightech one.  This level could be
one of my own. :)

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tuttie-Frutti, Medusa or HOM effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Quite  challenging,  lots of  Shotgun Guys,  Imps and Pigs.  A few
Cacos  and Barons.   Very  good  placed!  Alas,  there  is only  one
difficulty setting: Ultra Violence.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Exact amount of  ammo and health.  Delicious.  

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  95%

  What a nice thing! Download it immediately. There seem to be more
from Michael, so I have to look for them. :)
