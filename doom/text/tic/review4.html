 Review date: 1. May 95                              Review No.:   4
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Project: Recovery        Game: Doom
 Filename: AE_PROJR.ZIP             Size: 36k

   Author: Eric Sambach         e-mail: c9223826@cs.newcastle.edu.au

     Maps: E1M1                     Type: normal level
   Sounds: No
 Graphics: No
    Music: No

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Very well  thought trough.  The main 3 teleporters are moving down
after pressing switches. It's the main idea.
Nice traps & tricks. You always think 'now, this is a dead end'. But
you have to check rooms twice and don't forget to use the map.  Good
key-placement. Didn't find any secret...

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Most used texture is the 'ultimate' green castle brick.  Nice, but
this causes some  misalignments.  Especially windows and stairs have
misplaced textures. Doors, lifts and switches are well done.
Teleporters are also well done.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tutti-Frutties, Medusa-effects or HOMs.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Very challenging!  If you  like to kill a few  barons with shotgun
and chaingun...
The level contains  one  Cyberdemon.  Not easy to  kill (quite small
room), but possible. Good placed 'sudden attack' monsters!

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Well placed  ammo and  health  (only played UV) - sometimes  it is
quite hard.  The level contains  all weapons.  Only a few 'unusable'
things, could be more. (Lamps, Bodies etc.)

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  75%

  Worth downloading! A few minutes to align some textures and to put
some more things in it. Overall nothing new, but good work...
