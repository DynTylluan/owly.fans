 Review date: 14. May 95                             Review No.:  23
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Nine Hells               Game: Doom 2
 Filename: 9HELLZ.ZIP               Size: 70k

   Author: Unknown                e-mail: /

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal/deathmatch level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Nine big  rooms connected  with teleporters  and lots of hallways.
This level is meant to be a single, multiplayer and deathmatchlevel.
Lots of  the hallways  are looking  quite the  same,  kinda  stupid,
because you  never know  where you are.  Teleporters have  different
destinations - I hate that (you know, jump from 4 different sides in
the teleporter).  I think every bigger room contains a switch, which
opens a door to the end.  So you have to push  all switches to reach
the end. That's the main idea of the level, I think.
  No special light usage.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  A few  textures are  looking  misplaced.  The author  has a  kinda
strange taste placing gray ceiling parts in a brown room.  Therefore
the level looks a bit uncompleted.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tuttie-Frutti, Medusa or HOM effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Played in UV only. Not very hard, except one room with all kind of
monsters in it.  You will  get all  better weapons, like  the Rocket
Launcher or the Plasma Gun, so it is no problem to kill everybody.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo and health, you will never be stuck on both of it.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  60%

  Worth downloading.  Sometimes a bit boring, because  of the number
of hallways you have to pass,  but not the worst level. A bit to big
for deathmatch, unless you like fraggin' every 30 minutes. ;)
