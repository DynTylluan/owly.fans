 Review date: 29. May 95                             Review No.:  42
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: The Unholy Trinity       Game: Doom
 Filename: TRINITY2.ZIP             Size: 309k

   Author: Steve McCrea           e-mail: sm@eng.cam.ac.uk
           Simon Wall
           Elias Pappavassilopoulos

     Maps: E1M1                     Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: a lot of walls, sky
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  This is not  a Doom fighting level,  this is a  real classic.  The
authors designed the whole  Trinity College of  Cambridge with a lot
of (scanned?) textures.  The well  constructed interconnected rooms,
hallways and courtyards make the level a nice puzzle-level too. Good
light usage, but nothing special.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Only a few misaligned textures,  especially at stairs. But nothing
really bad, very good combinations of new and old textures.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Not very hard,  only a few places with some tougher enemies,  such
as a few Barons or Cacos. All in all well placed.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Good amount of  ammo and health,  only a few  other things - there
could be some more...

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  80%

  Worth downloading. A level rather for watching than fighting. Hard
work doing all the graphic-conversions, I bet. A must for every real
Doom-WAD-editor, to get an  idea of what can  be done! (Btw. running 
WARM over Trinity gives great speed-improvements...  I know, Trinity
was done in  september 94 and WARM  is much younger,  so the authors
could not use this tool - today this  would  give the ability to add
a lot of other Things, just to let you know.)
