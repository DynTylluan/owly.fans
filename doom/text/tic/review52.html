 Review date: 17. June 95                            Review No.:  52
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: The Monastary            Game: Doom 2
 Filename: MONASTER.ZIP             Size: 257k

   Author: Dominique Lavergne     e-mail:
                                        lavergne@dialup.francenet.fr

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: yes, from Heretic
 Graphics: yes, from Heretic
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A medium sized level,  well constructed and logically placed keys.
However,  the yellow  and red  keys  are a bit  too close.  The main
building is a church, looking very nice. Good light usage.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Almost every  texture is a texture from Heretic,  a few misaligned
textures, at stairs etc.  The level contains a few teleporters,  all
are misplaced. Could be much better - big walls are well done, small
corners and tiny walls are mostly misaligned.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Not  very hard,  a few Revenants  are the  hardest  enemies.  Well
placed and always fun to kill, nothing annoying.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Too much ammo and health I think, the whole level is full of Shell
Packs. This makes the level easier and fun, but it this is overdone.
Too few other things... I can't remember seeing any other things.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  75%

  Worth  downloading. Kinda easy and fast played level.
