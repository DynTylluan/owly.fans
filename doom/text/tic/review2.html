 Review date: 1. May 95                              Review No.:   2
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Unknown                  Game: Doom 2
 Filename: ENS.ZIP                  Size: 22k

   Author: Unknown                e-mail: Unknown

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: No
 Graphics: No
    Music: No

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Not that bad. Mostly build with cubes. Some windows, a lot of doors
and a few  teleporters.  Stupid ending, no  keys needed to end level.
Very illogical.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Computer center.  A lot of computer-walls,  very light.  No special
effects.  A lot of tricky misaligned  textures.  Repeating walls etc.
Doors are  okay.  Teleporters don't  have any good  floor  or ceiling
texture, just a blue one. Simple.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tutti-Frutties or HOMs.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Only tested UV.  Very hard.  Arch-Vile and Cyberdemon  included but
you don't need to kill them to end it.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  A LOT of 200% spheres. Too bad. At the starting  point is a plasma.
The level contains  all weapons.  No special things to make the whole
thing a bit 'real'. Just to get something.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  27%

  NOT worth downloading!  I recommend to put some  ideas down before
editing, to make the whole thing useful.
