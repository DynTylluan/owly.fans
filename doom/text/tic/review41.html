 Review date: 28. May 95                             Review No.:  41
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Outpost 21               Game: Doom 2
 Filename: OPOST21.ZIP              Size: 80k

   Author: Scott Amspoker         e-mail: scott@basis.com

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: sky
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Medium sized level,  originally designed  for Doom,  now converted
and extended for  use with Doom 2.  Nice construction  with a lot of
computer rooms, hallways, links and a cavern. Very well placed keys,
almost logically done. Good light usage, but nothing special.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  A lot of misaligned textures,  especially the part  with the brown
walls and green switches. A few strange combinations too. Floors and
ceilings are  often misplaced.  For being the  conversion of  a Doom
level, this could have been done much better.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs,  Medusa effects  or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Not very hard, only a few  Doom 2 enemies, usually Doom opponents.
But the end  is a bit harder,  because there are  most of the Doom 2
enemies. All in all too easy.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  A lot of  ammo and  enough health.  Too  few other  things placed,
except the cavern which is full of torches.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  72%

  Worth downloading.  Surely this one  was a very  famous level  for
Doom, but  the conversion is  not very  well done - why not  fix all
those misaligned textures?
