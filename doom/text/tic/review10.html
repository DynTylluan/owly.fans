 Review date: 2. May 95                              Review No.:  10
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: The Tiger Den            Game: Doom 2
 Filename: DM2TIGER.ZIP             Size: 56k

   Author: Jim Young              e-mail: jyoung@tiger.lsu.edu

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: No
 Graphics: No
    Music: No

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Medium sized level. Well planed, sometimes the rooms are a bit too
big.  Well placed keys.  Logical with a  few traps and  some hard to
manage places.  A few light failures.  The whole level could be much
more fine, looks a bit rough...
  One thing I didn't like: green waste is harmless, water hurts! :(

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Only a few  misaligned textures,  some light walls  have the wrong
size.  Some small,  tricky walls  are grey -  standard  wall of many
level-editors - not very nice.  Door sizes and teleporters are okay,
lifts could be done better. Ah, door's floors and ceilings are often
also not the best choice I think...

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tutti-Frutti, Medusa or HOM effect.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Enemies are well placed. A few traps, 'sudden attack' monsters and
some very  challenging on platforms above you.  Very hard to kill...
Overall a  good level for the  plasma gun - just fire into groups of
monsters.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Really enough ammo and too much health I think. It isn't that hard
at all.  Also good placed  ungetable things,  as mentioned  above it
looks a bit rough...

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  75%

  Worth downloading. The author should do some 'fine tuning'.
