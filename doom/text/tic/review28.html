 Review date: 16. May 95                             Review No.:  28
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Wet Work                 Game: Doom 2
 Filename: WETWRKD2.ZIP             Size: 71k

   Author: Zen Psychosis          e-mail: sychosis@usis.com

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Large level.  This doesn't mean a level with many, many rooms etc,
but one  with  a few  large  rooms,  connected with  (mostly)  small
hallways. I don't think the whole level is well thought through, but
the key-placement  isn't  that  bad.  All  three keys  are used.  No
special light usage, just light,  darker or dark rooms.  Use the map
to find the way to the keys.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Untidy.  Many, many misPlaced  and misAligned  textures.  Could be
much better.  Teleporters don't have the right floor,  just some red
or blue one. Doortraks are moving and windows are looking ugly.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tuttie-Frutti, Medusa or HOM effects. 

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Not very hard.  The level was converted  from Doom to Doom 2,  you
will  notice only  a few  enemies  from  Doom 2.  One Mancubus,  one
Revenant, a few Heavy Weapon Dudes and a Pain Elemental...

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo  and health.  Well  placed.  This means  you will find
something in every corner. ;)

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  50%

  Worth downloading only, if you really need a new level.
