 Review date: 17. May 95                             Review No.:  32
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Trilogy                  Game: Doom
 Filename: TRILOGY.ZIP              Size: 223k

   Author: Chris Spain            e-mail: cspain@eutelsat.fr

     Maps: E1M1-E1M3                Type: 3 levels
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: yep, windows, tv etc.
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  These three levels are kinda different from many others.  They are
not really Doom-levels... you will find many things from within your
own life.  Such as:  a bathroom, a metro,  an ambulance,  a disco, a
cementary,  some shops etc.  It's quite  funny  watching  all  these
rooms.  Even the key-placement  is good in almost  every level.  The
third level  is my  favorite,  it's the  cementary  with cool  light
effects. :)

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Alas, almost  every texture  is misaligned.  Aligning all textures
would make these  3 levels really amazing,  but this would be a very
hard job.  Although, the author  used good combinations  to make the
rooms more realistic.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, no Medusa effects, but a few Tutti-Frutties at stairs.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Not very hard, the levels are not  done to be a challenge I think,
at best the last level with a Cyberdemon...

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo and health,  good placed other  things too.  Sometimes
kinda macabre. :)

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  55%

  Worth downloading, it's rather for watching than fighting.
