 Review date: 24. June 95                            Review No.:  55
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Dungeon of Fear          Game: Heretic
 Filename: FEAR.ZIP                 Size: 44k

   Author: David Silver           e-mail: dsilver@gate.net

     Maps: E1M1                     Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A medium sized level with a few interconnected rooms. One big room
is the  center  of the  level,  if you  get  a key you  will  mostly
teleport back  and open the door.  Well placed keys.  A lot of areas
filled with water and a few traps.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Well designed. I noticed only  one misalignment,  however - why do
Heretic levels look more quality than a lot of Doom (2) levels?

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa or Tutti-Frutti effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Kinda easy, only 2 difficulty settings. Well placed tough.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo  and health.  You will  get most  of the  weapons,  so
killing all monsters is no problem.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  80%

  Worth downloading.
