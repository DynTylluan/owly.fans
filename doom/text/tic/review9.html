    Music: No

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A big labyrinth.  Very dark at all, it is designed like a cemetery
with lot of graves. Nice athmosphere, but illogical at all. You have
to push  same gravestones  to open doors,  activate lifts etc.  Nice
idea, but the whole level could be much more clear.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  It's  very dark,  so you will  not notice  most of  the misaligned
textures.   Sometimes the  author used  some very  strange textures,
looks ugly at some places. All looks quite the same, you HAVE to use
the map to know where you are.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tutti-Frutti, Medusa or HOM effect.  In fact, I didn't SEE any-
thing. ;)

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Enemies are well placed. They are at most attacking from behind or
from small vast places. Save often!

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  There are some  100% spheres and a LOT of  trees and other things.
Could be more ammo and little health packs.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  60%

  Worth downloading. But it's much to dark I think.
