    Title: Betray Level             Game: Doom 2
 Filename: BETRAY.ZIP               Size: 27k

   Author: Unknown                e-mail: /

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  The level is build up of blocks, only a few slanting walls.  A lot
of teleporters,  mostly without the original teleporter texture, but
with a normal  red or  blue floor.  A lot of  switches too,  opening
doors, walls or teleporters. Not very well thought through, you just
get a key  and will  find a door  to use  it with.  A labyrinth-like
level, although there are no labyrinths.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Only a few  misaligned textures,  the blue door  doesn't have  any
sign to show the needed key. Most teleporters doesn't have the right
floor. Beginner faults.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Not very  hard,  although  I played  it in Ultra Violence.  A good
level for the Plasma Gun,  a lot of ammo for it,  so some Mancubus',
Revenants and an Arch-Vile are easy to kill.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Things  are  restricted  to  monsters,  ammo and  health.  I don't
remember any other things. A lot of ammo and health though.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  57%

  Worth downloading, a very fast  completed level, although it isn't
a small one. Run, shoot, push 300 switches, run again etc...
