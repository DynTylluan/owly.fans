 Review date: 15. May 95                             Review No.:  26
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: "Argus" Episode, part 1  Game: Doom 2
 Filename: DM2ARGUS.ZIP             Size: 46k

   Author: Ty Halderman           e-mail: thldrmn@sam.neosoft.com
           Robert Taylor                  rtaylor@sam.neosoft.com

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Kinda one-way-level,  though it has a few rooms neighboring to the
main way.  This level really needs no keys, but two were used.  (You
know, run - get blue key - run - open blue door,  kinda stupid) Some
nice tricks,  like platforms  with  things  on it etc.  but  nothing
amazing. Some light effects, like cones of light...

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Kinda 'brutal'  texture placement,  but most  of them are aligned.
Some strange texture combinations, could be better.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tuttie-Frutti, Medusa or HOM effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Played in UV only. Too easy. Not very challenging at all - Guys,
Imps, Pigs. Boring.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo and health, too easy. Nice placed 'other' things.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  55%

  Worth downloading.  A bit boring.  I hope the  other WADs  of this
series will be BIGGER and better.
