 Review date: 7. June 95                             Review No.:  48
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Quest For Demonic Spirit Game: Doom 2
 Filename: SPIRIT2.ZIP              Size: 77k

   Author: Andy Badorek           e-mail:
                                    migru@vg3.chemie.uni-konstanz.de

     Maps: MAP01 (MAP02)            Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Medium  to large  level with a  lot of  ideas.  Very well  thought
through; a lot of interconnected rooms, stairs, hallways, lifts etc.
Well placed keys,  nice light usage.  A very  unique  trick to get a
blue sphere - you have to move a barrel to keep a door open...

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Almost every texture is aligned.  Some strange doors,  but this is
only a question  of getting used to.  The whole level  is kinda gray
with a few green (marble) walls.  Looks nice.  The texture-placement
on lifts is excellent.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Sometimes very challenging, mostly Shotgun Guys, Imps, Cacos and
Hell Knights were used. Very well placed.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo and health, sometimes you will run a while without the
latter, but this is just challenging. Only a few other things, could
be much more.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  80%

  Worth downloading. The WAD contains another map, but this is just
a rough beginning of another level.
