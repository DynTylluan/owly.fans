 Review date: 4. June 95                             Review No.:  46
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: The UAC Base             Game: Doom
 Filename: UACBASE.ZIP              Size: 142k

   Author: Brian J. Rowan         e-mail: blaze@oeonline.com

     Maps: E1M8                     Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: yes, a door, some walls
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Medium to big sized level with  a lot of hallways, computer-rooms,
switches and an evil end. Well placed keys and good light usage.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Almost every texture is aligned; the author put a lot of time into
alignment I think. Very well done.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Not very hard,  but well placed enemies.  At the end you will meet
a Cyberdemon and a Spider Mastermind at once.  But you don't have to
kill them to end the level. Overall well done.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo and health, could be some more other things.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  80%

  Worth downloading;  a very  interesting level,  there is a version
for Doom 2 out too.  Both files contain a dehacked file - it changes
some text data in Doom (2) only.
