 Review date: 24. June 95                            Review No.:  56
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: The Library Of Dis       Game: Doom 2
 Filename: LIBOFDIS.ZIP             Size: 53k

   Author: Guy Shelton            e-mail:
                                     gshelton@uhunix.uhcc.hawaii.edu

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A small to  medium sized  level with  a big outside-area.  But the
main action  takes places in one big  and 2 small buildings.  Mostly
symmetrically constructed.  Well placed keys,  but the blue key is a
bit difficult to reach. If you can't find the way, try your start-up
building.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Well designed, only a few misaligned textures.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa or Tutti-Frutti effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Quite challenging,  after killing the Baron near the pentagram you
will have to kill an Arch-Vile. Well placed enemies.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo and health.  Plasma and BFG  are not included.  Only a
few other things.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  75%

  Worth downloading.  It's the first  of a series,  hope to see some
others soon Guy!
