 Review date: 14. May 95                             Review No.:  22
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Straps 2.1               Game: Doom 2
 Filename: STRAPS21.ZIP             Size: 54k

   Author: Knut Arild Erstad      e-mail: ip103@ii.uib.no

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  A few labyrinths, hallways and rooms.  Nothing special and not the
best key-placement.  A very nice  trick near the end. :)  No special
light usage, just average  light and  a few  very light  rooms.  The
whole level isn't  well planed.  Some  'shoot-the-wall-to-open-door'
things are not very easy to find & understand - not very good.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Kinda strange and ugly sometimes, but not 'misaligned', just some
curious combinations. Could be much better.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tuttie-Frutti, Medusa or HOM effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Played in UV only.  Not the easiest level, sometimes you will need
some weapons hidden in secrets.  But well placed enemies, not all in
one room. However, nothing special.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Good amount of health and ammo.  There are some  rooms quite over-
loaded with things, other rooms are empty...

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  65%

  Worth downloading. It's the author's first level, so I think it is
very good for this time, but just average considering other WADs.
