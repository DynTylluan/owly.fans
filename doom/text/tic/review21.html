 Review date: 7. May 95                              Review No.:  21
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Nimble                   Game: Doom
 Filename: NIMBLE.ZIP               Size: 35k

   Author: Myles Williams         e-mail:
                                williams@martha.utcc.utk.edu

     Maps: E1M1                     Type: normal level
   Sounds: no
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  This is a very old WAD, July 94, but it's very good for this time.
Good key-placement,  a very skilfulness WAD.  Balance across several
blocks and walls.  Different  lights or light effects  are not used.
But the level contains invisible stairs!  Very good, but not easy to
find. A small level.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  A bit to  colored (I think  every textures  is used  at least  one 
time), but quite good aligned. Only a few misaligned textures. 

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No Tuttie-Frutti, Medusa or HOM effects.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Played in UV only. Not very tough... but sufficient.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo  and health.  It's a  small level,  so you can't place
much more  things around.  I noticed  some  flying trees -  not very 
well done.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  65%

  Worth downloading, but to small. However, take a look at it!
