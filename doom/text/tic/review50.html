Something of note

Twilight Lab, as made by Andy Badorek, is a Doom WAD that was made in 1995 as a standalone level. That same year, it would be donated to Memento Mori, a collaborative project that was, in part, made by The Innocent Crew - the same people who wrote these reviews.

This is, in fact, the second review of an Andy Badorek WAD by The Innocent Crew, the first being Quest For Demonic Spirit.

 Review date: 11. June 95                            Review No.:  50
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Title: Twilight Lab             Game: Doom 2
 Filename: TWILAB.ZIP               Size: 243k

   Author: Andy Badorek           e-mail:
                                    migru@vg3.chemie.uni-konstanz.de

     Maps: MAP01                    Type: normal level
   Sounds: yes, weapons, screams (optional)
 Graphics: no
    Music: no

[ Level construction ] ----------------------------------------------

  Andy is also  the author of  Spirit (2) - here we  got a sequel of
his style.  Very well  constructed  and  mostly in  technic/computer
design.  Many,  many rooms hallways,  interconnected places,  lifts,
stairs etc. -  a large  level.  All three  keys  were used  and well
placed.  Good light usage,  maybe some dark areas are missing.  Some
interesting secrets.

[ Texture design ] --------------------------------------------------

  Very well aligned, I didn't see any misalignments.  Often it seems
a bit 'unedited',  because of the  mostly gray style  (the gray ones
are standard  textures of  some editors).  But overall it looks very
matching.

[ Bugs ] ------------------------------------------------------------

  No HOMs, Medusa effects or Tutti-Frutties.

[ Enemies ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  Quite difficult due to  suddenly attacking monsters and a few very
nice traps.  Many Shotgun Guys,  Heavy Weapon Dudes and Imps.  Fewer
Demons, Cacos and Hell Knights. Well placed.

[ Things ] ----------------------------------------------------------

  Enough ammo  and health.  Too few  other things,  some  rooms  are
looking a bit too empty.

[ Scoring ] ---------------------------------------------------------

  82%

  Worth  downloading.  It was very  nice playing  this level,  maybe
the light is overall a bit to light. Play it in any case.
